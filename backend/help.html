Common Questions

  • How do I use the health prediction feature?

    Simply fill in the necessary health data such as age, cholesterol level, blood pressure, etc., and the system will generate predictions based on this information.

  • What kind of predictions does the system make?

    The system predicts risks for conditions such as heart disease, hypertension, and cancer, based on your provided health data.

  • Is my data secure?

    Yes, we take your privacy seriously and ensure that all data entered is securely stored and not shared with any third parties.
