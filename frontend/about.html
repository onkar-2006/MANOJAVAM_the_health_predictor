About Health Prediction Dashboard

This platform uses artificial intelligence to predict potential health risks based on medical and lifestyle data. It aims to empower individuals to take proactive steps toward maintaining their health and preventing chronic diseases.

Our system analyzes various health metrics like blood pressure, cholesterol, age, and lifestyle factors to help predict the likelihood of serious health conditions such as heart disease, cancer, and hypertension. Our goal is to provide personalized insights and recommendations that help users make informed decisions about their health.
